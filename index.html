Finacial Tech and Mobile Money

Mobile Money

  • NEIPlus in Northern Nigeria aims to increase the number of students, especially girls, enrolled in school.
  • It also wants to improve the reading outcomes for those students.

The Challenge

  • Staffing teachers in the remote areas of Nigeria is difficult, paying them is even harder!
  • Little to no access to traditional financial institutions

The Solution

  • Mobile money is popular in Western Africa
  • Works across mobile networks
  • Large network of agents able to assist and facilitate transactions
  • Digital allowing for tracking of payments and accounting
  • Helps prevent fraud and other crimes

Blockchain: Voting Pilot

The Project

  • Internal vote to test out applicability of blockchain-based voting systems in out of country votes (e.g. Somalia)
